Here' Why Selling Through An Agent Might Not Be Your Best Option:

Let say you own a house worth $400,000. 
You have a $320,000 loan on the property.
So you have $80,000 in Equity.
6% of $400,000 equals $24,000 in agent fees.
However $24,000 in agent fees is 30% of your Equity.
    ($80,000 x 30% = $24,000)
In other words, your $80,000 in equity is now just $56,000.
